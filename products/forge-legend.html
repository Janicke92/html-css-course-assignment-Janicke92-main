In this latest instalment in the epic saga of the kingdom of the Fireborn you play as Princess Emmber. She is the last of hope to save the kingdom from the endless darkness that looms in the west. With your trusted companion, Ink, the black wolf, you need to gather all the missing soul shards before the darkness envelopes the kingdom.

The saga continues 10 years after “The Rise of Darkness” where the hero of the Fireborn, Lynx, is captured by the forces of Glum. Without their hero, who was destined to defeat the darkness, the kingdom turns to their princess. It seems like a hopeless quest but Emmber can’t turn her back on her people. She seeks help from the only one she can trust, the great wizard Lumin. There the journey for the missing soul shards begins.

  • Discover all the hidden secrets of the kingdom of the Fireborn. Treasures and collectibles are found throughout the kingdom. Some behind puzzles and some behind terrifying foes.
  • Meet the people of the Kingdom. Through brilliant side quests and exploring you get to know everyday heroes, people effected by the darkness and maybe you even find love.
  • Journey through the amazing landscapes and villages you know from previous games and see how they have changed with the threat of the darkness. In this open world there’s no limit to when and where you can travel.
  • Acquire new skills in magic and combat as Emmber evolves from a quiet and forgotten girl to the hero no one believed she could become. But at what cost?

“You must know, Ember, if you go down this path, you will never be the same...”
