Our Vision

We strive to offer a wide variety of games. From indie 2D platforms to the biggest AAA games at a competitive price. When we first launched in 2012 we were the first online store to offer users to resell their finished games and to buy used or new games all in one place.

Our team is made up of people who are passionate about gaming and about making gaming available to as many people as possible. We see gaming as a way to build friendships across the globe and find other people with shared interests.
